Learn to pray Maghrib

Maghrib

Sunset Prayer

Maghrib, performed at sunset, contains three units. It reminds us to appreciate the day-night transition.

Before Praying,

You must:

  • Perform the Wudu
  • Face the Qibla
  • Ensure your attire is clean and covers the Awrah
  • Ensure that the place where you are praying is clean and free from impurities
  • Have the intention (Niyyah) to pray

Start The Prayer

" Allahu Akbar "

" الله أكبر "

Standing (Qiyam)

Stand upright facing the Qiblah, make Niyah (intention in your heart) for the intended prayer (salah).

Recite "Al-Fatiha"

بِسْمِ اللّٰهِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ اَلْحَمْدُ لِلّٰهِ رَبِّ الْعٰلَمِیْنَ(1) الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ(2) مٰلِكِ یَوْمِ الدِّیْنِﭤ(3) اِیَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَ اِیَّاكَ نَسْتَعِیْنُﭤ(4) اِهْدِنَا الصِّرَاطَ الْمُسْتَقِیْمَ(5) صِرَاطَ الَّذِیْنَ اَنْعَمْتَ عَلَیْهِمْ غَیْرِ الْمَغْضُوْبِ عَلَیْهِمْ وَ لَا الضَّآلِّیْنَ(7)

“A 'udhu billahi-minash-Shaytanir-rajeem. Bismilla-hir-Rahmaanir-Raheem. Alhamdu lillahi Rabbil-'alameen. Ar-Rahmanir-Raheem. Maliki yawmid-deen. Iyyaka na'budu wa iyyaka nasta'een. Ihdinassiratal-mustaqeem. Siratal-ladheena an'amta 'alayhim, ghayrilmaghdoobi 'alayhim walad-dalleen.” Ameen (Surah Fatiha 1:1-7)

Recite any Surah (ex: "Al-Ikhlas")

بِسْمِ اللّٰهِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِیْمِ قُلْ هُوَ اللّٰهُ اَحَدٌ(1) اَللّٰهُ الصَّمَدُ(2) لَمْ یَلِدْ وَ لَمْ یُوْلَدْ(3) وَ لَمْ یَكُنْ لَّهٗ كُفُوًا اَحَدٌ(4)

“Bismilla-hir-Rahmaanir Raheem.” “Qul huwal-lahu ahad. Allahus-Samad. Lam yalid wa lam yuulad. Wa lam yakul-lahu kufuwan ahad.(Surah Ikhlas 112:1-4)

Ruku'

" Allahu Akbar "

" الله أكبر "

go into bowing (Ruku'), and say this 3 times:

3x سُبْحَانَ رَبِّيَ الْعَظِيمِ

3x " Subhaana Rabbiyal Azeem "

Qauma

" Allahu Akbar "

" الله أكبر "

After raising the head from Ruku, (i.e., in the Qauma position), say:

سَمِعَ اللَّهُ لِمَنْ حَمِدَهُ

" Sami-Allahu liman hamidah "

Then, say:

رَبَّنـا وَلَكَ الحَمْـدُ حَمْـداً كَثـيراً طَيِّـباً مُـبارَكاً فيه

"Rabbanaa wa lakal-hamd, hamdan katheeran tayyiban mubaarakan feeh"

Sajdah

" Allahu Akbar "

" الله أكبر "

While going down to Sajdah, say this 3 times:

3x سُبْحَانَ رَبِّيَ الأَعْلَى

3x “ Subhaana Rabbiyal-a'la ”

During prostration (sajdah),

Seven parts of your body must touch the ground:

  • Your forehead along with the tip of the nose
  • Right hand
  • Left hand
  • Right knee
  • Left knee
  • the bottom of your toes (Right foot), all pointing in the direction of the Qiblah.
  • the bottom of your toes (Left foot), all pointing in the direction of the Qiblah.

Getting up from Sajdah

" Allahu Akbar "

" الله أكبر "

While rising from Sajdah, say:

اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَارْحَمْنِي وَاجْبُرْنِي وَاهْدِنِي وَارْزُقْنِي

“ Allaahum-maghfir lee, warhamnee, wahdinee,
wajburnee, wa'aafinee, warzuqnee, warfa'nee. ”

OR

رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي

“ Rabbighfir lee, Rabbighfir lee! ”
